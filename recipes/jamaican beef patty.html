Jamaican Beef Patty

The Ingredents

Pastry

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup shortening
  • ⅓ cup water

Filling

  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • ½ cup beef broth
  • ½ cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
